### Funkcjonalności
* Rozdzielczość: 480p
* System bezprzewodowy
* Dostosowana do działania w dzień jak i w nocy (~0.1 lux)
* Kompatybilna z wieloma pojazdami (pikcu-up, sedan, SUV)
* Linie pomocnicze + możliwość wyboru jednej 6 ich konfiguracji
* Może być użyta jako kamera cofania, kamera przednia lub zastępnik lusterek bocznych
* Możliwość ustawienia jasności/kontrastu

### Zalety
* Łatwa instalacja
  > For the price, this is a great product! One of the things I love most about this kit is how easy it is to install. The camera and display screen both have wireless transmitters, so there's no need to run any cables through the car. The camera also has a wide-angle lens that provides a clear, unobstructed view of the area behind the car, and the display screen has a high-resolution display that shows the camera feed in crisp, clear detail.

### Wady
* Jakość obrazu nie jest najlepsza. Jest rozpikselowany.
  > Instal was simple. While my Volvos wiring was different colors than the instruction manual the camera came with, it wasn’t hard to figure out. The picture quality is average at best. Very pixilated, lags a bit. If it has an auto exposure setting it’s basically non existent so if there is a glare, visibility will be difficult. Night time visibility is surprisingly good. The backup camera did fail once but they replaced it ASAP and linking the new camera to the monitor was a breeze.

  > The screen is smallish and the video quality is very laggy. Often loses video stream completely for a minute or 2.

* Brak czujnika, który informowałby o zbliżaniu się do przeszkody
  > A very nice addition to any car without a factory-installed backup camera. The only thing missing is a motion sensor to alert you when you might hit something
